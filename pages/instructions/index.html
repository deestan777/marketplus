Easy trading & Just in 2 steps

1

Simple registration

To make your experience more comfortable, TradersChain has worked hard on providing its clients with a secure withdrawal method. Withdrawal requests are quick at TradersChain. All you need to do is follow the simple steps listed below:


— Login to your trading account and register your withdrawal.

— Please note that withdrawals are credited back to you via the original method of deposit. For example, if your initial deposit was via credit card, then the removal is credited to you via the same manner with surplus funds (if applicable) credited to your nominated Bank account.


Example:

— Initial deposit $1000 via credit card

— Withdrawal $1500

— Therefore $1000 will be refunded back to the same credit card, and $500 will be refunded back to your nominated Bank account.

Segregated Client Accounts:

In the financial trading industry, this account is being used to hold your funds separately from the broker’s funds. This ensures that your capital is safeguarded by world-renowned credit institutions and can be easily identified.


To Deposit:

You will be required to present proof of your credit card by providing us with a recent credit card statement and a copy of the Credit Card.


The copy of your Credit Card (front and back sides) must show:

— Front Side – Your name, expiration date, first 6 digits and last 4 digits of the card number

— Back Side – Your signature *please black out the CVV2 number


Please note that a restriction on Credit Card deposits will remain on your Trading Account until proof of the Credit Card is presented. You may only deposit via Credit Cards that have been accepted by TradersChain.
